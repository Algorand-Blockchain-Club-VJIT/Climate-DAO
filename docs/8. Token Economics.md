# 8. Token Economics

## 8.1 Economic Overview

The AI-G-DAO token economy is built around Climate Impact Credits (CIC), creating a sustainable ecosystem that rewards environmental action while enabling democratic governance. Our tokenomics design incentivizes long-term participation, quality climate projects, and community growth while maintaining economic sustainability.

## 8.2 Token Supply & Distribution

### 8.2.1 Dynamic Supply Model

Unlike traditional fixed-supply tokens, CIC operates on a **dynamic supply model** where new tokens are minted based on verified environmental impact, creating direct correlation between token issuance and real-world climate benefits.

## 8.6 Long-term Economic Sustainability

### 8.6.1 Sustainable Growth Model

#### Phase 1: Bootstrap (Year 1)
- **Focus**: Community building and initial project funding
- **Metrics**: 1,000+ active users, 100+ projects, $1M+ carbon impact
- **Economics**: High rewards to incentivize early adoption
- **Sustainability**: Foundation funding and initial token sales

#### Phase 2: Growth (Years 2-3)
- **Focus**: Scaling impact and user adoption
- **Metrics**: 10,000+ users, 1,000+ projects, $10M+ impact
- **Economics**: Balanced rewards with increasing self-sustainability
- **Sustainability**: Transaction fees begin covering operational costs

#### Phase 3: Maturity (Years 4+)
- **Focus**: Global climate impact and self-sustaining ecosystem
- **Metrics**: 100,000+ users, 10,000+ projects, $100M+ impact
- **Economics**: Fully self-sustaining with diverse revenue streams
- **Sustainability**: Treasury surplus enables expanded impact
